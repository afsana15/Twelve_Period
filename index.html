About Us

12 Period is a student-led initiative within Enactus Exeter, dedicated to ensuring that everyone—regardless of socioeconomic background—has access to period products and the education needed to use them confidently. Our project works closely with schools, homeless shelters, and charities to distribute sustainable period pads while educating both men and women on menstrual health. Every product purchased through our partners contributes to a “buy one, give one” model, allowing essential donations to reach homeless women facing unique challenges.




Why Join 12 Period?

By joining 12 Period, you’re not just gaining valuable experience; you’re part of a movement that’s changing lives. As a team member, you’ll help create meaningful impact by breaking the stigma around menstruation, improving access to necessary hygiene products, and raising awareness within the community. This project is a fantastic addition to your CV, plus you’ll join a network of socially-driven leaders as part of Enactus Exeter. You’ll also play a role in educating others through resources like QR-coded pads that link to our bespoke informational website.
